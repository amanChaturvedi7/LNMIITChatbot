About

The LNM Institute of Information Technology(LNMIIT), is a deemed university located in Jaipur, India, on an 100-acre campus. The institute is public-private partnership venture between the LNM foundation and the Government of Rajasthan and operates as an autonomous non-profit organization. On this chatbot you can get answers of all your pre-admission/admission related queries, and there is a forum on which you may have an interaction between other people who have same queries as of yours. Technically speaking it is a Java based web application, and database used is MySQL.
